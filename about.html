About Us

At Deeper Life Bible Church Kent Region, we are more than just a community—we are a family united by faith, love, and a shared commitment to spiritual growth. Whether you're new to the area, seeking a deeper connection with God, or looking for a vibrant community to belong to, we warmly invite you to join us.

DLBC Kent Region is a dynamic and diverse congregation rooted in the teachings of Jesus Christ. Our mission is to lead people into a transformative relationship with God, nurturing them through sound biblical teaching, heartfelt worship, and practical discipleship. We are part of the larger Deeper Life Bible Church network, known globally for its unwavering dedication to holiness, evangelism, and Christian service.

What To Expect

When you visit DLBC Kent Region, you will find a welcoming environment where everyone is valued. Our services are characterised by powerful worship, impactful preaching, and a sincere pursuit of holiness. Whether you are new to the Christian faith or have been a believer for years, you will find teachings that challenge, inspire, and encourage you to grow in your walk with God.

Join Our Community

DLBC Kent Region is a place where you can build meaningful relationships and grow spiritually. We have a variety of ministries and programs tailored to meet the needs of all ages and stages of life— from children's ministry to youth groups, men's and women's fellowships, and more. There is a place for you here. We believe everyone has a unique role to play in the body of Christ.
